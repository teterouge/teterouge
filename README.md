# Richard Brion

Product builder working at the intersection of **AI systems, product strategy, and emerging technology**.

Most of my work focuses on taking ambiguous problems and building the **first working versions of systems** that eventually evolve into real products.

This usually means translating early ideas into functioning architectures — prototypes, evaluation frameworks, and tooling that help teams move from concept → implementation.

My background spans:

- AI product development
- early-stage product incubation
- enterprise data systems
- advanced technology domains including AI, autonomy, robotics, and quantum-adjacent systems

I tend to approach problems as a **builder first** — writing prototypes, building architectures, and experimenting with systems to understand what actually works in practice.
